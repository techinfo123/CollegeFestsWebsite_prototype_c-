Title: Basics of Digital Marketing: Understanding the Fundamentals

Date and Time: August 5th, 2023; 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM

Location: Jaypee Institute of Information Technology, Noida, India

Objective: To provide an introduction to the basics of digital marketing, including its principles, strategies, and tools.

Workshop Overview:
The workshop will be a beginner-level session, where participants will learn about the fundamentals of digital marketing. The workshop will cover the basics of search engine optimization (SEO), social media marketing, email marketing, and content marketing. Participants will also learn how to measure the success of their digital marketing campaigns using analytics and data-driven insights.
