Topic: "The Ethics of Biotechnology"
Date: September 10, 2023
Venue: JIIT Jaipur Campus
Speaker: Dr. Anjali Singh, Professor of Biotechnology at University of Delhi

Description: JIIT is honored to welcome Dr. Anjali Singh, a renowned expert in biotechnology and ethics.
 Dr. Singh is a professor at University of Delhi and has conducted extensive research on the ethical considerations surrounding biotechnology.
  In this talk, she will discuss the challenges and opportunities presented by biotechnology and how to ensure that it is used ethically.
